Welcome to Sekhon Billiards Academy

At Sekhon Billiards Academy, we don’t just play — we create champions, build focus, and nurture a love for the game. Located in the heart of Amritsar, near Nexus Mall, our academy offers a professional, friendly space for everyone — from first-time players to skilled cue artists. We believe billiards is not just a game, but a craft that teaches discipline, patience, and precision. With expert coaching, tournament-standard tables, and a supportive environment, we help players grow — both in skill and confidence. Our training programs, casual play sessions, and regular tournaments ensure that every visit is meaningful. Whether you want to learn, practice, or compete, Sekhon Billiards Academy is where your journey begins. It's more than just a club — it's a community where passion meets purpose. Come, experience the spirit of the game.
